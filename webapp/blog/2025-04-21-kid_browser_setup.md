# Introduction



<br/>
No browser is 100% safe. But there are ways to make your browser safer for kids. The goal is to create a safe browsing environment that allows kids to explore the internet while minimizing risks.

<br/>

It's also important to note, that every kid is different. Some kids are more tech-savvy than others, and some may be more susceptible to online dangers. So, it's essential to tailor your approach to your child's needs and maturity level.
